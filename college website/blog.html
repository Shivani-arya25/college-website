Our criteria & facilities

Elgibility

The candidates must have passed 10+2 from the Madhya Pradesh Secondary Board or any equivalent exam from a recognised
They must have secured a minimum aggregate of 45% of marks (SC/ST candidates the criteria is 40%).
They must have studied Physics and Maths as compulsory subjects with Chemistry/ Biology/ Biotechnology/Computer Science as the optional subjects in their 10+2.
The candidates must be residents of MP and must fulfil the domicile requirements of MP as specified.
The candidates are required to clear the JEE Main exam, which is conducted by Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

our campus

This college was established by government of Madhya Pradesh in November 1981 keeping the industrial development of Bundelkhand region in view and named it Government Engineering College Sagar and the name has been modified as Indira Gandhi Engineering College Sagar later on. The college is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (A I C T E) and all the courses are approved by AICTE as per mandate. .

CULTURAL ACTIVITIES

The cultural committee of the college encourages the students to take part in a wide variety of cultural competitions such as singing, dancing, debates, creative writing etc. and holds various cultural activities throughout the year

SPORTS ACTIVITIES

The college has setup a “Gymkhana Committee” to manage the sports activities at the college and encouraging the students to participate in the activities outside the campus.

NCC

College is equipped with several infrastructural facilities for NCC training programme and provides ample opportunities to the cadets by way of conducting firing camps, drill practices, personality development and voluntary service programmes.
